ABOUT- SANA SHAHEEN COLLEGE HUBBALLI

MISSION & VISION

Our Mission

To engage, educate and empower wards enabling them to seize the world of opportunities manifested in the form of free education across various domains in state owned academic centers of excellence.

Our Vision

To identify, tap and nature the innate potential in every child to shape next genaration that's succussful, socially responsible and reveres the noble tenets of islam to imbibe the true concept of wholesome living.



Objectives

1. To make quality education affordable to maximum number of students from all strata of the socity.


2. To inculcate the concept of team spirit in every child to help build a stronger nation.


3. To equip every ward with survival skills to face the bigger challeges thrown by life.


4. To encourage every student to believe in self and leverage self conviction.


5. To instill in every child a strong sense of partriotism and national pride.




OUR PHILOSOPHY

Shaheen is the sum total principles and values espoused by Allama Iqbal.The generation next would do well to
imbibe the noble qualities advocated and practiced by Shaheen as part of its broader philosophy.The governing
philosophy which influences the operational dynamics at Shaheen is driven by a specific set of lofty attributes aimed
at grooming a near perfect future generation.

Our Philosophy

1.High Aspiration-(Oonchi Udaan)

2. Clear Vision- (Tez Nigah)

3. Preference for Solitude -(Khilwath Pasand)

4. Untiring Effort -(Aashiyana Nahin Banata)

5. Self Relience - (Dusron ka Shikar nahin Khata)
